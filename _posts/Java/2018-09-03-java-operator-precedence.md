---|---  
postfix | `_expr_++ _expr_--`  
unary | `++_expr_ --_expr_ +_expr_ -_expr_ ~ !`  
multiplicative | `* / %`  
additive | `+ -`  
shift | `<< >> >>>`  
relational | `< > <= >= instanceof`  
equality | `== !=`  
bitwise AND | `&`  
bitwise exclusive OR | `^`  
bitwise inclusive OR | `|`  
logical AND | `&&`  
logical OR | `||`  
ternary | `? :`  
assignment | `= += -= *= /= %= &= ^= |= <<= >>= >>>=`
